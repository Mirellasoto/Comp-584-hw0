Instructure 

      Instructure Holdings, Inc. is an educational technology company based in Salt 
    Lake City, Utah, United States. It is the developer and publisher of Canvas, a 
    web-based learning management system (LMS).
      The company was founded in 2008 by two BYU graduate students, Brian Whitmer 
    and Devlin Daley. Its initial funding came from Mozy founder Josh Coates, who 
    served as Instructure's CEO from 2010 to 2018 and chairman of the board through 
    2020. (wikipedia).
